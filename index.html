My main research interests lie in cosmological simulations of the universe. I am currently using the Illustris simulation suite to analyze metallicity gradients of simulated galaxies to measure how they vary with mass and redshift. These results then will be compared to observational data, and I will attempt to deduce how well an observational study could observe these trends, given the current paradigm.
